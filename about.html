Cryptoloser

Losers from all walks of life have entered Losercoin, and it’s time for them to enter the public field of vision. Let’s look for the footprints of those busy workers in the all-profession series of Losercoin NFT, and learn about those people who sweat and enjoy working every day, and give recognition to those silent efforts at work. We shall salute to all the workers, and show them more respect and more gratitude!

The Losercoin all-profession series NFT is a special NFT work created by Losercoin community artists to salute to workers in all walks of life. The total number is 10,000, which includes 100 occupations and 5 types of rarity blind boxes.

Support

Loser bridge is a cross-chain bridge that allows exchange of crypto assets (e.g., fungible/non-fungible tokens, stablecoins) between Ethereum, Binance Smart Chain and Matic blockchains. Loser bridge UI is accessible at https://bsc.loserbridge.org/ and https://matic.loserbridge.org/

Loser bridge code is open source, you can find it on GitHub: https://github.com/cnmozzie/ethhmy-bridge.frontend

Farms

$LOWB is the native token of (Loserswap) app.loserswap.org, a decentralized finance (Defi) yield aggregator on Binance Smart Chain. You could use the Pools to stake and earn tokens with a not-so-high APR. You could also stake Liquidity Pool (LP) tokens to earn loser rewards in return.

To visit our Loser Farms, please go to https://app.loserswap.org/
